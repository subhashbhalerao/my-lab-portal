About Lab Logbook

  1. Student must do entry in Lab Log Book register
  2. Student must do all entries correctly in logbook, i.e. sn, system name, sap id, student name and sign
  3. Student must ensure of doing entry in logbook before leaving the lab
  4. It's students whole responsibilty to do entry in lab logbook

About handling computers

  1. Student must start the computers after instructed by faculty
  2. Student must shutdown the computers properly before leaving the lab
  3. Student mus handle the computers carefully
  4. Student must not move or bend computer angles and positions
  5. Student must not put any thing on the computer table except pen, pencil and notebook etc
  6. Student must keep bags at one place of the lab instucted by faculty
  7. Student must keep water bottles down the computer table
  8. Before student sits at the computer, please turn on backside power buttons and check the network cable connected properly
  9. If student find anything unusual with the computers, immediately inform to the faculy or lab assistant.

About Lab cleanliness

  1. Student must not bring eatables in the lab
  2. Student must not make the lab untidy by throwing the papers, pen, pencils etc in the lab
